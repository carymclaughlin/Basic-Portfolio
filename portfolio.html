About Me


My introduction to web development came about 10 years ago when I learned some of the basic structures of HTML. I made minor changes to the company website for my employer, updating links, creating new pages, updating employee profiles. I found it interesting but I didn't expect that I would imerse myself in programming.

I'm very happy that I have! This field exactly reflects my passion for learning and being creative. I am looking forward to spending the rest of my life pioneering in new technologies!

My background is in finance. I spent years as a salesmen, then transitioned to being an operations manager. I will be happy being a dev, but I imagine that my ability to communicate, my leadership, and my ability to see the big picture will ultimately place me in a team management, or department management role. I need to get further immersed in code and the precise uses of different programming elements before I can excel in this type of role, but ultimately my ability to use perspective is always valued by my employer.
